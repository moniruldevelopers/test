Teacher Rules

  • Teacher Qualification: Teachers must possess the necessary qualifications and expertise in the subjects they intend to teach.
  • Professional Conduct: Teachers are expected to maintain professionalism in all interactions with students and fellow educators.
  • Respect for Diversity: Teachers should foster an inclusive and respectful learning environment, respecting the diverse backgrounds and perspectives of students.
  • Original Content: All teaching materials and content shared by teachers should be original or used within the bounds of copyright law.
  • Non-Discrimination: Discrimination on the basis of race, gender, religion, or any other personal attributes is strictly prohibited.
  • Timely Communication: Teachers should respond to student inquiries and messages in a timely manner.

Areas of Expertise

  • Subject Expertise: Teachers must specify the subjects or topics they are qualified to teach.
  • Certifications: Teachers should list any relevant certifications or qualifications in their respective fields.
  • Teaching Experience: Teachers should provide information about their prior teaching experience, including the duration and any notable achievements.

  • Continual Learning: Commitment to staying current in their field of expertise and adopting best practices in education.
